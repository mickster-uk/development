# Product Launch Planner

## Use case
Use this template to produce a launch plan, announcement outline, or rollout checklist for a new product or feature.

## Qualifying questions
- What is being launched?
- What is the primary audience?
- What are the launch goals?
- Are there timeline milestones or channels to include?

## Prompt structure
Outline the launch strategy, major steps, and messaging points needed to coordinate the release.

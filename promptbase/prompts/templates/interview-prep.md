# Interview Preparation Coach

## Use case
This template helps generate interview questions, answer preparation, or coaching scripts based on a candidate profile and role requirements.

## Qualifying questions
- What role is the candidate preparing for?
- What is the target audience or interviewer style?
- Which strengths or experiences should be highlighted?
- Are there any specific questions you want to practice?

## Prompt structure
Use the details to create a focused interview preparation guide, practice questions, and suggested responses.

# Email Response Assistant

## Use case
Use this template when you want to generate a clear, professional email in response to a brief description of the topic and desired tone.

## Qualifying questions
- What is the purpose of the email?
- Who is the recipient?
- What tone should the email use (friendly, formal, direct)?
- Are there any key details or outcomes that must be included?

## Prompt structure
Use the information above to craft a concise email response that addresses the recipient and clearly states the next steps.

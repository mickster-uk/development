# Marketing Copy Brief

## Use case
This template is good for generating persuasive product or campaign copy when you have a target audience, key benefits, and a desired messaging style.

## Qualifying questions
- Who is the target audience?
- What problem does the product or service solve?
- What tone should the copy use (energetic, trustworthy, inspirational)?
- What action should the reader take?

## Prompt structure
Create a short marketing message that highlights benefits, addresses the audience, and ends with a clear call to action.
